Ordering

To place an order:
1. Browse products and add items to your cart
2. Click on the cart icon and proceed to checkout
3. Enter your shipping details
4. Select payment method and complete payment
5. You'll receive an order confirmation email

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact customer support immediately if you need to make changes.

Yes! We offer free gift wrapping for all orders. You can add a personalized message during checkout. The gift will be beautifully packaged with our signature gift wrap and a message card.

Shipping & Delivery

• Free shipping on orders above ₹999
• Flat ₹50 shipping charge for orders below ₹999
• Express delivery available at additional cost

Delivery times vary by location:
• Metro cities: 2-4 business days
• Tier 2 cities: 3-6 business days
• Other locations: 4-7 business days
• Express delivery: 1-2 business days (additional charges apply)

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status in your account or using the tracking link provided.

Payments

We accept:
• Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
• UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
• Net Banking
• Cash on Delivery (for eligible orders)
• Wallet payments (PhonePe, Paytm, Amazon Pay)

Absolutely! We use secure SSL encryption and PCI-compliant payment gateways. We never store your credit card information on our servers. All transactions are processed through certified payment partners.

Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for orders up to ₹5000. For COD orders, we may require a phone verification call to confirm the order.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Products must be:
• In original condition
• Unused and undamaged
• With all tags and packaging intact
• Accompanied by the original invoice

To initiate a return:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order you want to return
4. Click "Return Item" and follow instructions
5. Or contact our customer support for assistance

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned product. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
